Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes your email address when you create an account, and expense data that you choose to log including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect sensitive financial information such as credit card numbers or bank account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide solely for the purpose of providing and improving the Spendly service. Your expense data is used to generate summaries, charts, and insights about your spending patterns. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your data with third parties. We may use third-party service providers to host our application or send service-related communications, but these providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time. You can export your expense data or delete your account from your profile settings. If you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email or through a prominent notice on our website. Your continued use of Spendly after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us.
